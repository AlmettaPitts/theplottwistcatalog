# ✨ Welcome to The Plot Twist Catalog™

> *"What if another life is already waiting for you?"*

The Plot Twist Catalog™ is an immersive digital experience that invites people to reimagine their quality of life through storytelling, coaching, community, and intentional travel.

Rather than functioning as a traditional website, this project was thoughtfully designed to blend education and entertainment (edutainment), helping visitors explore what it might look like to intentionally create their next chapter.

Whether someone is seeking rest, clarity, a fresh perspective, or the courage to embrace meaningful change, this experience is designed to meet them with curiosity, possibility, and hope.

---

# 🌍 About the Project

This repository contains the source code for **The Plot Twist Catalog™**, an interactive web experience created by **Almetta Pitts, MSW, LICSW(A)**.

Visitors are guided through a story-driven journey that introduces two coaching experiences:

* **Domestic Reset™**
  *A three-session group coaching experience designed to help participants pause, reflect, and intentionally reconnect with themselves.*

* **International Escape™**
  *A four-session group coaching experience designed for people exploring global travel, life transitions, and the possibility of creating a different way of living.*

Throughout the experience, visitors are encouraged to imagine what their next chapter could become while exploring travel opportunities, community partnerships, and practical coaching resources.

# 💡 Project Philosophy

The Plot Twist Catalog™ was built around a simple belief:

**Life doesn't always change because we arrive somewhere new. Sometimes it changes the moment we give ourselves permission to imagine another possibility.**

This project encourages visitors to pause long enough to ask thoughtful questions about the life they're living and the life they're hoping to create.

It is an invitation to explore what might be waiting on the other side of intentional change.
